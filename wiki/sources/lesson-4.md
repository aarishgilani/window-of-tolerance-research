# Lesson 4 — The Three Circuits and Neuroception

**Type:** Course lesson note  
**Ingested:** 2026-06-13  
**Source:** Heptabase Card Library

## Summary

- Deep dive into each of the three polyvagal circuits with biological mechanisms
- Ventral Vagal Complex (VVC): myelinated, originates in Nucleus Ambiguus, enables the Social Engagement System
- Sympathetic circuit: metabolic CFO, functional fallback when Ventral brake releases. High-performance states = Ventral-Sympathetic blend
- Dorsal Vagal Complex (DVC): unmyelinated ancient brake, triggers conservation-withdrawal with endogenous opioid release
- Neuroception: subcortical threat surveillance scanning external, relational, and internal domains — including moral alignment as a survival strategy

## Key Claims

- VVC originates in the **Nucleus Ambiguus** (brainstem); the vagal brake provides rapid, precise cardiac regulation
- Social Engagement System (SES) links vagus to cranial nerves controlling face, middle ear, and throat
  - Middle ear in Ventral state: filters for human speech frequencies, attenuating low-frequency "predator" sounds
  - Voice gains melodic prosody; eyes show vibrant expression in safety
- "Felt Safety" is a **bottom-up neurobiological state** — cannot be achieved through cognition alone if Ventral hardware is offline
- Flow and Play are **Ventral-Sympathetic Blends** — high energy held within a Ventral anchor
- Pathological SNS: chronic low-level threats (emails, self-criticism) keep the system mobilized → tunnel vision, shallow thoracic breathing
- DVC releases **endogenous opioids** during shutdown to mask pain — explains emotional numbness
- Distinguishing regulated calm vs. False Calm: Dorsal = foggy/heavy/distant; Ventral = present/expansive
- Neuroception scans three domains: External (lighting, exits), Relational (facial expressions, prosody), Internal (heart rate, gut tension)
- **Moral alignment as survival strategy**: individuals align with group consensus to avoid the autonomic distress and metabolic cost of social ostracism
- Higher interoceptive awareness → greater sensitivity to being "out of sync" with the tribe → can increase social conformity pressure
