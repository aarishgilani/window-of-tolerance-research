# Getting Started on Mobile

👋 Welcome to Heptabase!



Here are a few easy steps to get you started:

- [ ] **Tap anywhere in a card** to start typing

- [ ] **Add a to-do** **in a card** by typing `[]` + space, or use `/` to open the quick menu

   - [ ] **Check all to-dos** from the **Task** (you'll find it in your navigation bar)

- [ ] **Save a webpage or video** to Heptabase using your phone's Share menu

- [ ] **Record a Voice Note** by tapping the `＋` button at the bottom-right

   - [ ] After recording, head to the **Journal** to find your recording and transcript

Want to explore more?

- [ ] Create a new **whiteboard** for a topic you're interested in exploring

- [ ] Download **Heptabase** on desktop or laptop to visually organize your knowledge and insights



That's it! If you need help, visit **Home > Settings** anytime. We're here to support you!
