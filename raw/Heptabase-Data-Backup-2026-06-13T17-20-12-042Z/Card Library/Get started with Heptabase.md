# Get started with Heptabase

Heptabase is designed specifically for **students,** **researchers, and lifelong learners**. 

We built this product to help you master anything you study and do your best research with AI.



**How to get started with Heptabase?**

1. **Create a whiteboard** for your learning topic. Add related materials as "cards" — PDFs, YouTube videos, images, research notes, and journals.

   + Two questions to ask yourself

      1. What topic do I want to learn or research?

      2. What materials do I need to learn or research this topic?

   + Watch how it works

      [https://youtu.be/q2wloOL3fao](https://youtu.be/q2wloOL3fao)

2. **Use AI** to discuss your whiteboard or specific cards. AI can answer questions and help you build a comprehensive understanding of your content.

   + Watch how it works

      [https://youtu.be/0I5Fpdzn0Ag](https://youtu.be/0I5Fpdzn0Ag)

3. **Read and annotate** these cards using the built-in PDF reader, video player, and text editor. Select confusing content to ask AI questions, and organize key ideas into note cards.

   + Watch how it works

      [https://youtu.be/ZbDRkAlg1f0](https://youtu.be/ZbDRkAlg1f0)

   - [Advanced article tutorial](https://medium.com/heptabase/the-best-way-to-use-ai-for-learning-762c3467bdf1)

4. **Organize your notes** as they grow. Use connection and section tools to visually map relationships between concepts.

   + Note-taking Tips 

      Start with all notes on one card. Once it's rich enough, break it into smaller concept cards and arrange them on your whiteboard to visualize their relationships.

   + Watch how it works

      [https://www.youtube.com/embed/HgvR2QkfwG0](https://www.youtube.com/embed/HgvR2QkfwG0)



**Heptabase currently offers two subscription plans—Pro and Premium:**

- **Premium plan**: Get 10x monthly AI usage quota of the Pro plan and unlimited PDF uploads. Ideal for users who heavily rely on AI for learning and research and want extensive use of the most advanced AI reasoning models.

- **Pro plan**: Suitable for users who use AI features less frequently and want to use Heptabase as their primary note-taking and knowledge base software.

   In fact, even before launching any AI features, Heptabase won the Product Hunt 2023 Personal Productivity Tool of the Year award, making it a visual note-taking software particularly well-suited for students and researchers.
