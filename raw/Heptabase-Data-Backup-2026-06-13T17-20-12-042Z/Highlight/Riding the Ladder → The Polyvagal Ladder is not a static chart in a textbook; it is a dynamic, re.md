# Highlight

The Polyvagal Ladder is not a static chart in a textbook; it is a dynamic, real-time map of your nervous system's response to the world. If neuroception (which we covered in the last part) is the "security camera" system that scans for cues of safety or danger, the ladder is the "elevator" that moves you to the appropriate physiological floor to handle what the cameras see.
