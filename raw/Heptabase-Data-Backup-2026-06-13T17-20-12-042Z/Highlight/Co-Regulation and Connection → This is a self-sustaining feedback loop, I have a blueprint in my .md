# Highlight

Without the "mirror" of another regulated nervous system, our internal "security cameras" (neuroception) tend to default to a state of high-alert (Sympathetic) or a protective numbing (Dorsal). Co-regulation is the process by which one person’s Ventral Vagal state serves as an anchor for another's.
