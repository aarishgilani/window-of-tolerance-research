# Highlight

When you practice **extended exhale breathing** (e.g., inhaling for 4 seconds and exhaling for 8), you are essentially maximizing the "braking" phase of the cycle. You are spending twice as much time in a state of vagal dominance as you are in sympathetic activation. For a student like you who uses breathwork, understanding RSA transforms the practice from "calming the mind" to "manually slowing the heart via the vagal cable."
