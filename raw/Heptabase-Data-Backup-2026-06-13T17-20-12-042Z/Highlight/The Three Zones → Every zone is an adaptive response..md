# Highlight

Every zone is an **adaptive response.**

- If a car is swerving toward you, you *want* to be kicked into hyper-arousal immediately. You need that surge of adrenaline to jump out of the way. 

- If you are undergoing a surgery or a traumatic event where you cannot escape, hypo-arousal is a biological mercy—it numbs the pain and creates a "mental distance" that helps you survive the unsurvivable.
