# Highlight

Karl Friston, one of the most cited neuroscientists in the world, developed the **Free Energy Principle** to explain this. In simple terms, the brain is trapped in a dark, silent box (your skull). It has no direct access to the outside world or the inside of your body. All it receives are electrical "pings" traveling up the pathways we studied in Part 4.2. To make sense of these pings, the brain uses its past experiences to construct a model of what is *likely* happening.
