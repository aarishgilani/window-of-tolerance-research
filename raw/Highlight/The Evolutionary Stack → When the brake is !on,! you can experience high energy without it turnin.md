# Highlight

When the brake is "on," you can experience high energy without it turning into panic. For example, during intense physical training or competitive sports, you are using a high degree of Sympathetic mobilization. However, if your Ventral Vagal system is still active (the "Safety" circuit is engaged), that mobilization feels like "play" or "flow" rather than "threat." This is how your nervous system mixes states—a concept we will explore in later topics.
