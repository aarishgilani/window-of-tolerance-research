# Highlight

Neuroscientist Antonio Damasio calls these **"Somatic Markers."** They are interoceptive "tags" that the brain attaches to memories and concepts. By sharpening your interoception, you become more "literate" in the language of your shadow. You can catch the somatic marker of "shame" the moment it arises in your body, allowing you to stay curious and regulated rather than being overwhelmed by the emotion.
