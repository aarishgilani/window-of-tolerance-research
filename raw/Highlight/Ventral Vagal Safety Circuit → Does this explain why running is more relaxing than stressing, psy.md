# Highlight

In reality, the nervous system is much more efficient. Instead of hitting the gas (Sympathetic activation), it simply **lifts the Vagal Brake**. By reducing the inhibitory signal from the Ventral Vagus, the heart rate naturally rises to its intrinsic 100-120 bpm range. This allows for low-level mobilization without the "metabolic cost" of adrenaline and cortisol.
