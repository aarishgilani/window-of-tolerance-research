# Highlight

This contraction squeezes a "fresh" supply of oxygenated blood into the circulatory system. It’s an **autotransfusion**. For a diver, this extends their time underwater. For a person using this for regulation, it provides a subtle surge of oxygenation that can clear "brain fog" and provide a moment of heightened clarity amidst a crisis. It is the body's way of saying, "If we're going to survive this, I’m giving you everything I’ve got."
