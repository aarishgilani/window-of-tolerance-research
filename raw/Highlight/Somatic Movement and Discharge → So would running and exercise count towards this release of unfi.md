# Highlight

Humans have the same hard-wired mechanism, but we have been socially conditioned to suppress it. We have been taught that shaking is a sign of "losing it." When we suppress the tremor, we trap the energy in the body, which can eventually push us out of our Window of Tolerance and into a state of chronic hyper-arousal or, eventually, Dorsal Vagal collapse (shutdown).
